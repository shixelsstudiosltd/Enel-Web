How to Play

How to play online

Registration

Players will be required to register online prior to creating an Enel account. If a player has not previously created an account, the website will automatically prompt the player to do so. Once completed, players will be provided with options on how to credit their Enel Games Wallet. Once players have credited their wallets, they are able to access and play any game of their choice.

Crediting / Loading Wallet

Gaming Voucher
Players can credit their ENEL Games Wallet by purchasing a ENEL Games recharge voucher. Players should scratch the back of the card to obtain a pin. The pin should then be inputted into the website as prompted.
Bank transfer
Players can credit their ENEL Games Wallet with funds from their bank accounts (via XXXX) using their debit cards. Once players have provided the relevant bank details and selected the amount they wish their Wallets to be credited by, they press ‘complete transaction’. Once the transaction has been authorized, the amount will be automatically reflected in their ENEL Games Wallet.
SMS

Players can credit their ENEL Games Wallet by purchasing a ENEL Games recharge voucher. Players should scratch the back of the card to obtain a pin. Players should then text ‘xxx-message-xxx’ to the ENEL Games short code ‘xxxxx’.

As an example, if the voucher pin was 123456789101, players would text the following on their phones to the ENEL Games short code ‘xxxx’: ‘Load.123456789101#’ and then press ‘send’.

Agent/3rd party retailer
Players can credit their ENEL Games Wallet through an ENEL Games agent or 3rd party retailer. Players should provide the agent or 3rd party retailer with the cash amount they want to credit into their accounts and their phone numbers. Once the agent or 3rd party retailer has completed the transaction, players will receive a SMS confirmation from ENEL Games confirming the amount their wallet has been credited. The SMS receipt is confirmation that players’ account has been credited. ENEL Games therefore encourages all players to ensure that the text message from ENEL Games is received prior to leaving the agent or 3rd party retailer. Alternatively, if for any reason the SMS confirmation was not received, players can call the ENEL Games customer service number (+234 00 000 00) to confirm whether their accounts have been credited.

Choosing a game

Xtra Star

The size of the jackpot is directionally proportional to the proceeds collected under the draw. As a result, the jackpot will translate to millions and millions of Naira, which one lucky player can win!

To win the jackpot, players must select 6 numbers out of 49 and match all 6 numbers with the 6 numbers drawn randomly by ENEL Games !

There are major cash winnings for 3, 4 and 5 ball winners

The more numbers you match the more you win

There’s a second chance draw as well when there’s no jackpot winner, we give out major cash prices to a number of players who played the game for that particular draw

The winners are randomly selected from all the participating players using the unique player ticket ID

The Xtra Star draw will take place every xx-day-xx evening and will be shown live on television. Where there is no jackpot winner, the 2nd chance draw will take place automatically after the Xtra Star draw.

Purchasing a ticket

Select the Xtra Star link on the front page of the ENEL Lotto website. Select the number/s of your choice and bet types.

Confirm the ticket purchase by selecting ‘Place Bet’.

Once ‘Place Bet’ has been selected, you will be taken to a payment screen that will give you the following payment options:

Pay from wallet: If you have already have an account with ENEL Games and have funds in your wallet, you can select this option.

Load voucher: If you do not have funds in your account or have not registered, you can enter the pin code from the back of a ENEL Games Game voucher here.

Once the appropriate payment details have been entered, you will be requested to review your purchase details. Once you are happy with it, press ‘buy’ to confirm the ticket purchase. Your purchase is completed and you will be sent an email or text confirmation verifying your purchase.

Accessing Results

ENEL Games offer players several platforms to check results. These include

Online – Current and past results are available at www.enelotto.com

Customer service – Results can be accessed by calling the customer service line on 2340000000. Call costs vary according to network operator services.

SMS – Results can be accessed by SMS in the format; ‘Command.Name of Game’. For example, ‘RESULTS.XS’. Texts costs N10.

ENEL Games outlets – Results can be accessed from any ENEL Games shop.

ENEL Games mobile application – Results can be accessed via the ENEL Games mobile application.

Cashing Out

Players can select to claim their winnings through:
ATM
Players who opt for this option are sent a unique transaction code, which they need to input into the ATM machine to collect their winnings. Transaction fees may apply
Transfer to bank
Winnings can be collected over ENEL Games accredited banks via mobile money or over the counter transactions. Players will be sent a unique reference number, which they take to any of these banks where winnings will be immediately paid out after presenting a valid ID. Transaction fees may apply.
Cash at ENEL head office
Winnings can be claimed at the ENEL Games headquarters on upon presentation of a valid ID and confirmation text message.(minimum payments may apply)

Key Functions

Check balance
Players will be able to check their available balance by logging into the ENEL Games gaming website and selecting their ENEL GAMES Wallet to view how much money is in their gaming account. ENEL GAMES Wallet is located under “My Account”
Check historical results
These results can be checked by logging into the ENEL Games gaming website, selecting the game of choice and selecting the results tab to view historical results. Results can also be accessed by SMS in the format; ‘Command.Name of Game’. For example, ‘RESULTS.XS’. Texts costs N10.
Check historical ticket purchases
To view past ticket purchases, players are to log into the ENEL Games gaming website, select their ENEL GAMES wallet and click on the “past ticket purchases”.
Check status of a game
To view the status of a game, players are to log into the ENEL Games gaming website, select the ENEL GAMES wallet and it will display the ticket. Click on the individual ticket and view the status of the played game.

Qualification of 2nd Chance Draw

Second chance draws are strictly available for the Xtra Star game.

In the event there is no jackpot winner, ENEL Games will run a 2nd chance lucky draw where multiple winners are randomly selected for cash prizes ranging from Thousands to Millions, depending on the size of the jackpot. Players therefore have two chances of winning a cash prize: through the normal Xtra Star draw and the 2nd chance lucky draw!

The 2nd chance lucky draw will be based on players’ ticket ID numbers. Each ticket bought by players will come with a different ID number. Players that buy a ticket for Xtra Star are automatically entered into the ENEL Games 2nd chance lucky draw.

As winning the 2nd chance lucky draw is based on selecting a players’ ticket IDs, the chances of winning the 2nd chance lucky draw dramatically increase the more tickets players acquire! ENEL Games therefore encourages all players to acquire as many tickets as possible so as to maximize their chances of winning the jackpot or the 2nd chance draw.

The Xtra Star draw will take place every xx-day-xx evening and will be shown live on television. Where there is no jackpot winner, the 2nd chance draw will take place automatically after the Xtra Star draw.
