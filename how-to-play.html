How to play online

Registration

Players will be required to register online prior to creating an Enel account. If a player has not previously created an account, the website will automatically prompt the player to do so. Once completed, players will be provided with options on how to credit their Enel Games Wallet. Once players have credited their wallets, they are able to access and play any game of their choice.

Crediting / Loading Wallet

Gaming Voucher
Players can credit their BIG GamesBIG Games Wallet by purchasing a BIG GamesBIG Games recharge voucher. Players should scratch the back of the card to obtain a pin. The pin should then be inputted into the website as prompted.
Bank transfer
Players can credit their BIG GamesBIG Games Wallet with funds from their bank accounts (via interswitch) using their debit cards. Once players have provided the relevant bank details and selected the amount they wish their Wallets to be credited by, they press ‘complete transaction’. Once the transaction has been authorized, the amount will be automatically reflected in their BIG GamesBIG Games Wallet.
SMS
Players can credit their BIG GamesBIG Games Wallet by purchasing a BIG GamesBIG Games recharge voucher. Players should scratch the back of the card to obtain a pin. Players should then text ‘Load.VoucherPin#’ to the BIG GamesBIG Games short code ‘20120’. As an example, if the voucher pin was 123456789101, players would text the following on their phones to the BIG GamesBIG Games short code ‘20120’: ‘Load.123456789101#’ and then press ‘send’.
Agent/3rd party retailer
Players can credit their BIG Games Wallet through a BIG Games agent or 3rd party retailer. Players should provide the agent or 3rd party retailer with the cash amount they want to credit into their accounts and their phone numbers. Once the agent or 3rd party retailer has completed the transaction, players will receive a SMS confirmation from BIG Games confirming the amount their wallet has been credited. The SMS receipt is confirmation that players’ account has been credited. BIG Games therefore encourages all players to ensure that the text message from BIG Games is received prior to leaving the agent or 3rd party retailer. Alternatively, if for any reason the SMS confirmation was not received, players can call the BIG Games customer service number (0700 4000 4000) to confirm whether their accounts have been credited.
