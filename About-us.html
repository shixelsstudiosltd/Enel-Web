About Enel Star

Who we are at Enel
At Enel-star we operate a huge Lotto in Enugu State that starts at ₦15,000,000 Star Xtra has gained national notoriety for consistently handing out huge jackpots. A total of seven jackpots of more than ₦300,000,000 have been won in its lifetime. Officially on sale in all 36 states, as well as Star Xtra tickets that can also be played online and through various ticket agents nationwide.

Our Games

Six Star Xtra

Six Star Xtra is the brand for our marquee parimutuel game based on players choosing 6 numbers out of a selection of 49

Five Star Lotto

Five Star Lotto is the brand for our other marquee parimutuel game based on players choosing 5 numbers out of a selection of 36

Corporate Social Responsibility

The real reason behind the lottery is that it is a great way to raise funds for worthy causes within the State. Through its gaming operations in Enugu State, Enel Star Lotto intends to raise a significant amount funds for reinvestment in the Enugu State community.

When you play one of our games, a portion of the money goes to fund youth sports in Enugu State. So you may not win the jackpot, but one of those young athletes might change the world.

When you play, they win!

How to Access Our Platform & Where to Find Us

You can access our platform through any electronic device that can connect to the internet or is sms enabled. These include devices powered by iOS, Android, Windows and Blackberry. The benefit of using sms protocol is that you do not have to have a data package on your device.

You can also play our games at various retail and agent outlets across Lagos State. More information on these outlets can be found by calling 0700-4000-4000.

You will also be able to learn how to play our games at these retail and agent outlets.
